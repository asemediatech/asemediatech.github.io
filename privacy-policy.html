Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 29, 2025

1. Introduction

ASEMedia Tech ("we," "our," or "us") builds applications to make your digital life easier. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications, with specific details for our app, GoTagihan.

2. Information We Collect

Our goal is to collect the minimum amount of data necessary to provide a functional and reliable service. The data we collect depends on the application you use.

Specifically for GoTagihan:

For the GoTagihan app, we do NOT collect personal identification information like your name, email address, or phone number, as the app does not require registration or login.

The information we process includes:

  • User-Provided Customer IDs: We process customer identification numbers (e.g., ID Pelanggan PLN, nomor BPJS Kesehatan) that you voluntarily enter into the app to check your bills. This information is sent directly to the public APIs of the respective service providers to fetch billing information.
  • Locally Stored Data (History): For your convenience, the Customer IDs and the results of your bill checks (riwayat tagihan) are stored only on your device's local storage. This data is not uploaded to our servers and remains under your control.

General Device & Usage Data (All Apps):

To improve app performance and stability, we may collect anonymous data, including:

  • Device Information: Anonymous information such as device type, operating system version, and screen size to ensure compatibility.
  • Usage and Crash Data: Anonymous analytics about which features are used most and automatic crash reports to help us identify and fix bugs. This is typically handled by third-party services like Google Firebase.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Core App Functionality: For GoTagihan, this means using the Customer ID you provide to fetch and display your billing information.
  • To Enhance User Experience: Storing your check history locally allows you to review past bills without re-entering data.
  • To Maintain and Improve Our Services: Analyzing anonymous usage data helps us understand user needs, fix bugs, and improve app stability.
  • To Provide Customer Support: If you contact us for support, we may use your provided information to assist you.

4. Data Security

The security of your data is a top priority. For GoTagihan, we have implemented specific measures to protect your information:

  • Local Storage: All sensitive data, including your Customer IDs and billing history, are stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this information.
  • Secure Transmission: When fetching data, we use secure protocols (HTTPS) to communicate with service provider APIs where available.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

Our applications may use third-party services for advertising and analytics. These services may collect anonymous data as described in their own privacy policies.

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements in the app.
  • Google Firebase: Used for anonymous analytics and crash reporting.

Furthermore, GoTagihan retrieves data from public APIs provided by or on behalf of service providers like PLN and BPJS Kesehatan. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top.
